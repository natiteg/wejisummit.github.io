The Ethiopian Full Gospel Believers' Church has its origins in a prayer conference held at the University of Addis Ababa in 1966.

Christ's vision, as outlined in the Bible, emphasizes love, compassion, forgiveness, and service to others. He taught that the way to salvation and eternal life is through faith in God and following his teachings, including the Golden Rule to "love your neighbor as yourself."
